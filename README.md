## Findings & Recommendations
### Customer Performance Report
#### Findings
+ Overall net sales growth from $87.5M in 2019 & 196.7M in 2020, to 598.9M in 2021, showing a 304.5 % growth from FY-2020 to FY-2021
+ Customer 'Nova' shows a unique growth rate of 2664.9%. This suggests either a new partnership or significant changes in sales strategies 
with the clients
#### Recommendations
+ Focus on high (having High Net sales) performers (Amazon, AtliQ e Store, AtliQ Exclusive) to ensure consitent revenue stream
+ Provide some offers in terms of discount, to high growth customers (Chiptec, Nova, Integration Stores) to help sustain their growth
+ Explore barriers for lagging in potential customers (Flipkart, E Bay, Leader, Sage), and implement strategies to accelerate their growth



### Market Performance vs. Target: For Various countries From FY-2019 TO fy-2021
#### Findings
+ The grand total for 2021, $598.9M is falling short by 54.9M from the target, resulting in an overall shortfall of -8.40%.
+ India & USA despite missing the targets by $9.6M (fell short by -5.59%) and $10.2M (fell short by -10.44%), had a massive conribution 
of net sales 161.3 M and 87.8 M respectively.
+ Other Notable countries like Canada and Spain, fell short by 12.63 %, 12.39 %
+ Poland is the consistent uner performer, lagging the most by -15.35%

#### Recommendations
+ India and USA are the major contributors. There should be strategic focus on these markets to bridge the shortfall gap
+ Investigate about high shortfall Markets like Poland, canada, to find the underlying issues
+ Revising targets based on realistic and achievable metric, can help in setting more practical goals
