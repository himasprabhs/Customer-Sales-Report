# Customer Sales Report


## 💼 About
- Domain: FMCG
- Function: Sales



## 🤔 Problem Statement

Decode sales data, Identifying trends, tracking important indicators for AtliQ Technology, 
to make better decisions, do customized offering, and become profitable to stay competitive in today's business world.

## Findings & Recommendations
#### Customer Performance Report:

##### Findings:
+ Overall net sales growth from $87.5M in 2019 & 196.7M in 2020, to 598.9M in 2021, showing a __304.5 %__ growth from _FY-2020 to FY-2021_
+ Customer __'Nova'__ shows a unique growth rate of _2664.9%_. This suggests either a new partnership or significant changes in sales strategies 
with the clients

##### Recommendations:
+ Focus on high (having High Net sales) performers __(Amazon, AtliQ e Store, AtliQ Exclusive)__ to ensure consitent revenue stream
+ Provide some offers in terms of discount, to high growth customers (Chiptec, Nova, Integration Stores) to help sustain their growth
+ Explore barriers for lagging in potential customers (Flipkart, E Bay, Leader, Sage), and implement strategies to accelerate their growth



#### Market Performance vs. Target: 
(For Various countries From FY-2019 TO fy-2021)

##### Findings:
+ The grand total for 2021, $598.9M is falling short by __54.9M__ from the target, resulting in an overall shortfall of __-8.40%__.
+ **India** & **USA** despite missing the targets by $9.6M (fell short by -5.59%) and $10.2M (fell short by -10.44%), are **a massive conribution 
of net sales 161.3 M and 87.8 M respectively**.
+ Other Notable countries like Canada and Spain, fell short by 12.63 %, 12.39 %
+ __Poland__ is the _consistent under performer_, lagging the most by -15.35%

##### Recommendations:
+ India and USA are the major contributors. There should be strategic focus on these markets to bridge the shortfall gap
+ Investigate about high shortfall Markets like Poland, canada, to find the underlying issues
+ Revising targets based on realistic and achievable metric, can help in setting more practical goals


>> By addressing these recommendations, the business can take better decisions to optimize their market performance in various countries.
